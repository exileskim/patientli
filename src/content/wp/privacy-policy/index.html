Privacy Policy for Patientli

Effective Date: February 4, 2025

Patientli is committed to protecting the privacy of your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.   

1. Information Collection:   

We collect the following information from users:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number (if provided).
  • Business Information: Details about your healthcare practice, such as practice name, number of doctors, website url, services offered, and location. This information is used to tailor our services and marketing efforts to your specific needs.

2. Use of Information:

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To deliver the services you have requested, such as website design, brand design, and marketing services.  
     
  • Marketing: To contact you with information about our services, promotions, and industry news that may be relevant to your business. We may use your business information to personalize these communications.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improving Services: To analyze website usage and customer feedback to improve our services and website.

3. Sharing of Information:

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with the following categories of recipients:   

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us with website hosting, data analysis, email marketing, and other services necessary for our business operations. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and only use it for the purposes we specify. 
     
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: If Patientli is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified via email and/or prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or control of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information   
     

4. Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include:   

  • Password Protection: Our website is password-protected. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): We encourage the use of 2FA for added security.
  • Secure Hosting: We use reputable hosting providers with robust security measures. Patientli partners with Flywheel (getflywheel.com) for hosting. 

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we use reasonable efforts to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.   

 
5. User Rights:

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@patient.li. We will respond to your request within 7 days.

6. Cookies:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your activity on our website. These technologies help us analyze website traffic, personalize your experience, and improve our services.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

7. Children’s Privacy:

Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.   

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy:   

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this website with a revised effective date. Your continued use of our website or services after the posting of the updated policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.   

9. Contact Us:

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@patient.li.
