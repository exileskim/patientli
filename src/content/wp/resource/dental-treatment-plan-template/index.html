Dental Treatment Plan Template

Help move high-value cases towards treatment acceptance with this free dental treatment plan template. Outline a treatment overview, share a timeline and detail costs in a professional and personalized format.
Download now

Our dental treament plan proposal template includes the following easy-to-customize layouts:

  • Treatment Overview Page: Explain the high-level goals and expected outcomes of treatment, along with a phased treatment timeline.
  • Clinical Findings Page: Share information and photos of the patient's current dental condition
  • Proposed Treatment Timeline: An overview of the major milestones in the patient's treatment plan along with dates.
  • Comprehensive Treatment Plan & Budget: An easy-to-understand breakdown of the specific treatment, dates and costs at each step of treatment
  • Payment & Financial Info Page: Set expectations around payment policies including accepted methods of payment, insurance coverage, financing options and more.
  • Helpful resources: Share educational blog posts or pre and post op instructions to help patients prepare for treatment
  • Signature Page: A place for patients to sign to accept treatment

Professionally present treatment proposals for large cases

Hate handing patients a print-out of treatment codes? Our free treatment plan template is a solution that’s sure to impress. It’s a beautifully formatted way to share treatment proposals with patients, especially those considering costly or complicated treatment plans like all on x or cosmetic smile restorations. 

Our template is easy to understand, displaying the patient’s full treatment plan in a breakdown by phase so patients can understand their full treatment roadmap– from what’s happening to when it’s scheduled and how much they should expect to budget for each phase of the plan.
