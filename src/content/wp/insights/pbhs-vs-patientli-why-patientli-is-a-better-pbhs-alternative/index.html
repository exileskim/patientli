Is Patientli a good PBHS alternative?

PBHS vs Patientli: Website Design

PBHS RevenueWell offers clients two different website approaches: a custom website solution with a $6,000 setup fee and more cost effective semi-custom templates, which are priced at $169/month. Their sales representatives actively promote their semi-custom sites as their standard offering. These templates can be lightly customized by the PBHS team during the onboarding process. Afterwards, clients are provided with login credentials and instructions to handle most design updates and maintenance themselves through DIY editing tools.

Their sales team explains that the website design process typically involves matching website colors to existing practice logos, which assumes practices already have professional branding. Practices who don’t or aren’t thrilled with their existing brand styles may be disappointed with the results as logo colors aren’t always suitable for website applications – they may lack proper contrast ratios for accessibility compliance or fail to work harmoniously across different web applications. 

If a client gets tired of their semi-custom website’s aesthetic, PBHS allows them to switch to different templates within their fairly extensive library of templates. For custom sites, a redesign would incur another $6,000 setup fee.

The verdict: PBHS semi-custom websites provide a decent, lower-cost option for practices who place a lower priority on design quality and uniqueness and who have the bandwidth and expertise to handle some parts of their setup and updates on their own. 

On the other hand, Patientli has a single pricing plan for websites that forms the foundation for its website-only Basic plan ($750/mo) and gives clients full access to the Patientli website system. Their website system differentiates on high-quality design and was built by designers that have worked for top agencies and technology companies. While their sites use a system of modules, reusable building blocks for commonly used sections like patient reviews, doctor bios, and more, their design system allows for infinite combinations of website sections, styles and more to achieve custom, high-quality designs quickly – delivered in as few as 2 weeks compared to PBHS 6 week to 6 month timelines. 

From a design perspective, Patientli’s website quality far exceeds PBHS’ offerings. Rather than adapting websites to existing logos, we design complete visual systems that work cohesively across all applications – from websites to business cards to social media, so you get more than a custom website. This integrated approach ensures your practice appears professionally everywhere patients might encounter it, with modern, polished designs, proper color contrast for accessibility and visual harmony across all patient touchpoints.

Compared to PBHS’ two website offerings, Patientli sites are more comparable in nature and quality to PBHS custom designs, though that isn’t even a fair comparison. Here are a few differences: 

  • Patientli sites are designed and updated for you by their team with free unlimited updates, so you’ll never be stuck trying to figure out how to update your site when a new team member is added or a new service becomes offered. 
  • Patientli’s websites are built on a framework that the company is constantly updating to stay up to date with the latest guidelines and trends around web accessibility, design, SEO and even AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Gemini and Claude. Their sites are designed to be the last website you’ll ever need to buy, breaking the web design cycle that mandates an expensive redesign every 2-3 years to keep up with changing technical standards and marketing demands.
  • Their Basic plan provides much more than a website. In fact, websites are just one part of their innovative Looks product, a brand solution that allows healthcare practices to access a growing number of top agency-designed brand styles that includes things like business cards, stationery, signage, patient giveaways and more– basically everything a modern practice needs to look polished and professional, standing out from the crowd to impress today’s discerning patients. Plus, Patientli allows their clients to restyle their website every two years by switching to a new Look, keeping them at the forefront of changing design trends– perfect for practices who want to come across as cutting edge or high-end. 
  • At its core, Patientli is a marketing company, so its websites are built to serve as a strong foundation for powerful digital marketing strategies, which are available in its higher-tiered plans. 

The verdict: If great design and marketing integration matters to you, and if you’re willing to meet a budget closer to a PBHS custom site in exchange for a full-service option, Patientli is worth considering. The key difference comes down to design quality and service delivery: PBHS RevenueWell provides tools and templates for practices to manage themselves, while Patientli handles all design work, updates, and maintenance professionally.

The verdict: PBHS’ branding services are offered as an afterthought to its websites and don’t seem to be where the company excels. Patientli’s brand design services lead the industry. Their branding services are deeply integrated into all of their packages, allowing practices to put their best foot forward to stand out from the crowd and attract the best patients.

What PBHS RevenueWell Offers

Protective Business and Health Systems (PBHS) has been around for a long time– founded in the 1970s as an accounting and payroll system and later adding web design to its offerings. PBHS was strategically acquired by RevenueWell in 2021 to create a unified platform that combines website services with practice management software. Recently, customers have complained that buying a website through PBHS opens the door to sales reps pushing other Revenuewell products. 

However, client satisfaction appears mixed. Their Yelp reviews average 1.9 out of 5 stars, and they currently hold an F rating with the Better Business Bureau. Employee reviews on Glassdoor average 3.6 out of 5 stars, with feedback citing customer satisfaction challenges and high employee turnover. Employee reviews mention that “the company is desperate to stay competitive but sacrifices customer satisfaction and isn’t receptive to employee burnout.”

Their software platform strength lies in practice management automation – appointment reminders, patient communications, and workflow optimization. 

PBHS RevenueWell operates as a large-scale service provider with established systems and processes. This scale provides consistency and comprehensive feature sets, though it may limit personalized attention compared to smaller, more focused companies.
