Patientli Launches Leading Healthcare Referral Program for Industry Professionals

March 2025 – Healthcare Marketing Industry News

Patientli, the specialized healthcare marketing company, today announced the launch of its healthcare referral program, offering commissions ranging from $750-$3,000 to consultants, practice brokers, and healthcare professionals who refer practices in need of marketing and branding solutions.

The healthcare referral program is designed to provide healthcare industry professionals with a reliable marketing partner they can confidently recommend to their clients, while creating a new revenue stream for their existing relationships.

Addressing a Common Industry Challenge

Healthcare consultants, practice management advisors, ownership transition specialists, and practice brokers frequently encounter clients who need professional marketing and branding support but lack the expertise to evaluate marketing providers or manage multiple vendor relationships.

“We kept hearing from industry professionals that they wanted to help their clients with marketing challenges but didn’t have the bandwidth to research providers, manage relationships, or ensure quality outcomes,” said Justin Bosco, founder of Patientli. “Our healthcare referral program gives them a turnkey solution they can recommend with confidence.”

Who Can Participate

The Patientli healthcare referral program is open to a wide range of healthcare industry professionals, including:

Practice Management Consultants who help practices optimize operations and growth strategies

Practice Brokers and Ownership Transition Consultants who facilitate practice sales and acquisitions

Fellow Doctors and Dentists who have colleagues struggling with patient acquisition, treatment acceptance rates, or unprofessional branding

Healthcare Industry Advisors who provide strategic guidance to practice owners

“Any professional who works with healthcare practices and wants to help them succeed is welcome in our referral program,” Bosco explained. “We’re particularly interested in working with advisors who understand that professional branding and marketing directly impact practice value and patient outcomes.”

Program Benefits and Commission Structure

Partners earn commissions equal to one full month of the plan their referred practice selects:

  • Basic Plan referrals: $750 commission
  • Starter Plan referrals: $1,750 commission
  • Growth Plan referrals: $3,000 commission

Commissions are processed automatically after the referred practice maintains their subscription for 90 days, ensuring partners are compensated for successful, long-term placements.

Tools and Resources for Partners

Patientli provides partners with comprehensive support materials to facilitate successful referrals:

Custom Referral Links that track referrals automatically and ensure proper commission attribution

Template Communications including email templates and conversation starters for approaching potential clients

Free Marketing Analysis Reports that partners can request and share with prospects to demonstrate value and identify specific improvement opportunities

Social Media Content that partners can share to establish themselves as healthcare marketing thought leaders

“We make it easy for partners to start conversations about marketing needs,” Bosco noted. “Many consultants want to help with marketing challenges but don’t want to become marketing experts themselves. Our tools let them provide immediate value while positioning Patientli as the execution partner.”

Focus on Practice Growth Outcomes

The healthcare referral program aligns with Patientli’s core mission of helping healthcare practices attract more patients, increase treatment acceptance rates, and build professional brand presence that reflects the quality of their clinical care.

“Our best partnerships happen when everyone is focused on the same outcome: helping practices grow,” said Bosco. “Partners aren’t just referring clients to a vendorâ€”they’re connecting practices with a growth system that addresses branding, patient acquisition, and treatment acceptance in an integrated way.”

Partners can refer practices facing common challenges including:

  • Low patient acquisition numbers
  • Unprofessional or inconsistent branding
  • Poor treatment acceptance rates
  • Ineffective websites that don’t convert visitors to patients
  • Reputation management challenges
  • Lack of online visibility in competitive markets

Quality Assurance and Client Experience

Patientli’s subscription-based model and transparent results reporting ensure that referred practices receive consistent, professional service that reflects well on the referring partner.

“When a partner refers someone to us, their reputation is on the line too,” Bosco acknowledged. “We take that responsibility seriously. Our goal is to make every partner look good for making the referral.”

The company’s “Looks” brand system and comprehensive marketing platform have helped practices like Fort Washington Dental Associates increase new patient acquisition from 15 to over 40 patients per month, providing partners with concrete success stories to reference in future conversations.

Getting Started

Healthcare professionals interested in joining the leading healthcare referral program can sign up at www.patient.li/partners. The program includes no upfront costs, minimum referral requirements, or ongoing obligations.

“We want this to be completely risk-free for partners,” Bosco said. “If you know a practice that needs better marketing, branding, or patient acquisition, we want to make it as easy as possible for you to help them while earning fair compensation for the referral.”

About the Partner Program Launch

The healthcare referral program launch represents Patientli’s commitment to building relationships throughout the healthcare industry ecosystem. By working with trusted advisors and industry professionals, Patientli aims to reach more practices that can benefit from professional marketing and branding solutions.

“The best referrals come from people who understand the practice and care about their success,” Bosco noted. “That’s exactly the kind of partners we want to work with.”

About Patientli

Patientli is a healthcare marketing company that helps dental and healthcare practices attract more patients through modern branding, comprehensive marketing strategies, and patient experience design. Founded in 2024 as a spin-off from Form + Function Agency, Patientli specializes in making enterprise-level marketing quality accessible to independent healthcare practices through its proprietary “Looks” brand system and transparent pricing model.

For more information about Patientli’s Partner Program, visit www.patient.li/partners.
