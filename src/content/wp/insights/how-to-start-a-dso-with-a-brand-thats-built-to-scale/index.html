Why Use Patientli for Your DSO Brand & Website Needs

1. Immediate professionalism

The creative process can be long, time consuming and expensive. Patientli Looks are a shortcut to making an impressive first impression for your DSO with potential patients and partners. A thoughtfully designed brand identity signals trust, expertise, and credibility. It also communicates your vision and ambitions in a way that lets potential partners and investors immediately know that you mean business.

2. Built for scale

Patientli Looks are crafted with growth in mind and created by a professional healthcare branding agency. Our team’s experience building brands for large organizations is incorporated into every Look, using thoughtful guidelines, templates and systems that the world’s largest and most innovative companies use to keep their brands professional and consistent as they grow.

Additionally, Patientli Looks integrate perfectly with our website system. Growing DSOs love the ability to use our system depending on their DSO brand strategy to either maintain brand consistency across all of their locations, or allow each location to choose a look that matches their needs. 

3. Launch faster than ever

Each Patientli Look includes a comprehensive suite of assets designed to keep your brand consistent across all of the patient touchpoints and marketing channels included in your Patientli plan. Looks can include designs for a logo, a complete website, professionally-picked colors, fonts and imagery, stationery like business cards and letterhead, signage, patient giveaways, social media and advertising assets. Just pick a Look you like and launch in as few as 14 days.

4. Domain names included

If you’ve spent any length of time brainstorming name ideas for your DSO, you’ve likely experienced the disappointment of finding that the website url for your idea is already owned by someone else. We’ve already done the work of securing domain names for each of our Looks, so you can get started without the heartbreak.

5. Customizable to Your Needs

While our brands come ready to use, they can also be customized to align with your unique practice goals and aesthetics, ensuring a perfect fit for your DSO.

6. Keep costs manageable

When designed from scratch, a world-class brand and website can easily run $25,000-$50,000, placing these services out of reach of many startups. With Patientli, you can keep costs manageable and pay as you grow with affordable monthly plans and a per-location payment structure.

7. Use a single Look for your whole DSO or allow practices to each have their own.

Whether you’re seeking to operate as a single branded DSO with multiple locations (think Tend or Dentologie) or allow each location to have a brand of its own (think unbranded DSOs like Heartland) Patientli can help you achieve efficiencies from your scale. Our branding and website infrastructure allows unbranded DSOs to allow each practice who joins to choose their own Look while maintaining consistent website content and layouts across your whole org. Branded DSOs can easily take advantage of our Looks for a consistent and clear national identity that’s ready to compete with the best of them.
